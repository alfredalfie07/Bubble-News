How to send money from Uganda to Kenya, Rwanda, Nigeria and Ghana

Are you in Uganda and need to send money to Kenya, Rwanda, Ghana, and Nigeria? Look no further than the Eversend app enabling you to send money to 5 African countries.

Learn More

There are 3 common ways to send money on Eversend.

  • Eversend to Eversend (Wallet to wallet):

    You can send money from your Eversend wallet to another wallet in 5 countries (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda, and Uganda), and the transfer is free and instant. So whether they are in-country transfers or cross-border transfers, customers are able to send money affordably.

  • Eversend to Mobile Money (MoMo)

    This transfer option allows you to send money from your Eversend wallet to a mobile money account in Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda, and Uganda. The recipient doesn’t have to be on Eversend.

    It is important to note that if the recipient is an Eversend user, this option will not be available as you are able to send directly to their Eversend wallet. They can then withdraw from their wallet to their mobile money account.

  • Eversend-to-bank

    Send directly to bank account
